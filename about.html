About People's Pride Trust

Empowering Lives, Building Futures

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by providing education, employment, and sustainable development solutions. We envision a world where every individual thrives with dignity and opportunity.

Our Key Initiatives

Education Support

Providing quality education for children in rural areas.

Women Empowerment

Training and empowering women to become self-reliant.

Village Development

Improving infrastructure, sanitation, and resources in villages.

Employment Generation

Creating job opportunities through innovative initiatives.

Founder's Message

"We believe that true progress happens when people are empowered to uplift themselves and their communities. Together, we can create lasting change." - Ashok Kola
